Overview

My interest in STEM fields started in elementary school and has been increasing ever since then; exploring and creating new technologies has and always will be a passion and a passtime for me. I also love learning how things (STEM related or not) work in general (Youtube tutorials take up a lot of my time).

WIPAC

Data Analyst

The Wisconsin IceCube Particle Astrophysics Center (WIPAC) is an astrophysics lab based out of UW-Madison. They collect and analyze data from the IceCube neutrino detector in the South Pole. My job was to analyze neutrino data (using primarily python) and develop datasets of neutrino “events” that the computer algorithms commonly misclassified (i.e. type, direction, energy, etc. of neutrino). By partitioning misclassified data it allows for improvement of computer classification algorithms as well as new potential discoveries.

RL Trading Bot

I’m currently working on applying reinforcement learning to financial trading in small cap stocks, forex, and cryptocurrency. Trading can be thought of as a game (with some pretty severe consequences). A bot can learn what action is best to take based on hundreds or thousands of input features. (I don't currently have a public repository for this).
